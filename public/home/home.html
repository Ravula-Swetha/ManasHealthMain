Mental health test, also known as a psychological assessment or mental health screening, is a tool used to evaluate an individual's emotional well-being, cognitive functioning, and overall mental health. These tests are designed to help mental health professionals, such as psychologists, psychiatrists, or counselors, gather information about a person's mental and emotional state.

An anxiety test is a diagnostic tool used to evaluate the presence and intensity of anxiety symptoms. Typically in the form of self-report questionnaires, these tests help assess emotional, cognitive, and physiological aspects. Consultation with a healthcare professional is recommended for accurate diagnosis and treatment.
A depression test is a screening tool designed to assess the presence and severity of depressive symptoms in individual. Typical administered through self-report questionnaires, these tests gauge emotional,cognitive, and physical aspects of depression.
An ADHD test is a diagnostic tool to assess attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder symptoms. Usually comprising self-report questionnaires and behavioral assessments, it helps evaluate attention span, hyperactivity, and impulsivity. Professional consultation is essential for accurate diagnosis and tailored intervention strategies.
An eating disorder test is a screening tool to assess patterns of disordered eating behaviors. Typically involving self-report questionnaires, it helps identify symptoms like restrictive eating or bingeing. Consultation with a healthcare professional is crucial for accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment.

Stress is a physiological and psychological response to perceived threats or demands. It triggers the body's "fight or flight" reaction, releasing hormones like cortisol. Chronic stress can have detrimental effects on physical and mental health, leading to conditions such as anxiety, depression, and cardiovascular issues. Effective stress management involves adopting coping strategies, lifestyle changes, and seeking professional support when needed.
