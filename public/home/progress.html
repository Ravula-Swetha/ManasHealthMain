Your Mental Health Adventure

Yay, you made it to your special progress page! It's like your own magical map showing how awesome you're becoming. Buckle up for a fantastic mental health adventure filled with learning and growing!

Just like superheroes have their own journeys, you're on a quest to feel amazing inside and out. Along the way, you might find challenges, but remember, challenges make us stronger. Your courage is like a superpower that helps you face anything!

If you ever want to share your adventure or ask for help, talk to your trusted grown-ups or friends. They're like friendly wizards ready to guide you. Keep exploring, little adventurer! Your mental health journey is extraordinary, just like you.

Understanding Your Mental Health Results

Hello friend! Your mental health results come in different levels, just like different adventure stages. Let's explore each level together:

Normal:

Normal results mean you're doing fantastic! Your feelings and thoughts are in a great place. Keep up the good work by doing things you love and talking to people who make you smile.

Mild:

Mild results are like a gentle breeze reminding you to take extra care of your feelings. It's okay to feel this way, and you can make it even better by doing things that bring you joy and talking to someone you trust.

Moderate:

Moderate results mean your feelings are a bit like a rollercoaster. It's perfectly normal, and talking to a grown-up you trust, like a parent or teacher, can help you find a way to enjoy the ride.

Severe:

Extremely Severe:

Extremely Severe results are like a dragon to be tamed. It's a big adventure, but you have a team of grown-ups who care about you. Share your feelings with them, and they'll help you conquer any dragon and make everything better.

Every level is a part of your unique journey. You're strong, brave, and important. Keep shining bright!
